1. **What’s the purpose of a BMC?** (Learn the parts)
A strategic management tool to visualise, design, and test a business model. It's purpose is to map out key components, simplify planning and support decision making. It's most valuable for its simplicity and ability to align teams on shared vision, making it ideal for startups or businesses exploring new markets.
