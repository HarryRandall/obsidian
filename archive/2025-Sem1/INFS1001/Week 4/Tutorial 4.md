#### What's the purpose of digital transformation
- A change in how a firm employs digital technologies, to develop a new digital business model that helps to create and appropriate more value of the firm.
- Value creation and capture: increased value from information; multisided business models;
-  coordinated business models in networks; control of digital industry architecture.
