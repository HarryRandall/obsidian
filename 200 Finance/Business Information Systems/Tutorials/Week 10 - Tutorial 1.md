## Key Concepts

### 1. Purpose of Business Model Canvas

- A strategic management tool to visualise, design, and test a business model
- Its purpose is to map out key components, simplify planning and support decision making
- Most valuable for its simplicity and ability to align teams on shared vision
- Ideal for startups or businesses exploring new markets

### 2. Purpose of Systems Modelling

- Involves creating abstract representations of complex systems

### 3. Lewin's Change Management Model

**Unfreeze:**
- Let stakeholders know the current problems, why change is needed, what change is going to be
- Focus on the benefits/positives
- Reassure stakeholders with any concerns that they have
  - i.e., Reassurance about jobs - will retrain instead of getting in new workers

**Change:**
- Implement the aforementioned changes
- Make a specific schedule of how it will be rolled out

**Refreeze:**
- Stabilise the changes, help everyone adapt to their new roles

### 4. Leadership Styles

1. **Transformational** - Leadership style that inspires and motivates followers to achieve extraordinary outcomes
2. **Transactional** - Leadership style based on exchange relationships and contingent rewards

### 5. Role of the CIO

- Digital Transformational - The role has evolved to focus on digital transformation initiatives

### 6. Cyber Security Threats for Educational Institutions

- Manages lots of user data:
  - Grades
  - Salaries
  - Scholarships
  - Personal details etc
