## Key Concepts

### 1. Product (Content Strategy)

- Reduce some in-house production as their specialised tracking software has revealed lower retention rates in some countries
- Allocate these funds into new content acquisition
  - Specifically studios with strong production capabilities
  - Identify ways to streamline the production process and cut production costs while also improving content.

**Purpose**
- Improve quality of content and also improve customer retention whilst also reducing costs

**Actions**
- Allocate funds from previous films into new content acquisition

**Targets**
- New subscribers

### 2. Apple TV+

**Product / Services**
- Apple TV Box
- Customer service with genius bar

**Content creation / offerings**
- 45 + originally developed TV and Movies
- Existing content for rent or purchase

**Examples of Content/Files/Videos**
- Severance

**Plans**
- Individual
  - Apple Music, Apple TV+, Apple Arcade, iCloud+
  - 50GB of storage
- Family
  - Shared with 5 people
  - Apple Music, Apple TV+, Apple Arcade, iCloud+
  - 200GB of storage
- Premier
  - Apple Music, Apple TV+, Apple Arcade, iCloud+
  - Apple News+, Apple Fitness+
  - 2TB of Storage

**Quality / Awards**
- A number of Apple TV originals had received Golden Globe awards.
